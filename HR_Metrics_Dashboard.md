## Reporting Cadence Recommendation

| Frequency | What to Report |
|-----------|----------------|
| **Weekly** | Headcount, open positions, urgent turnover, attendance |
| **Monthly** | Turnover, time-to-fill, absenteeism, overtime, hiring pipeline |
| **Quarterly** | Engagement scores, training metrics, compensation analysis, diversity, retention trends |
| **Annually** | Full workforce review, pay equity audit, succession planning, HR budget vs. actual, strategic workforce plan |

---

## Pro Tips for Management Reporting

- **Always tie metrics to business outcomes** — e.g., show how turnover impacts revenue, not just the percentage
- **Use trend lines** — a single number is less useful than a 6-month or YoY trend
- **Benchmark externally** — compare against industry standards where possible
- **Segment the data** — break down by department, location, job level, and manager to surface actionable insights
- **Include cost impact** — translate HR metrics into financial terms (cost of turnover, cost of vacancy, etc.)
